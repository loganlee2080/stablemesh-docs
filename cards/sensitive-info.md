# View Sensitive Info

Your full card number, expiry date, and CVV are hidden by default for security. You can reveal them whenever you need to make a purchase.

---

## How to View Card Details

1. Go to **Cards** and click the card you want.
2. In the detail panel, click the **eye icon** (查看详情 / View Details).
3. The card panel reveals:
   - **Full card number**
   - **Expiry date**
   - **CVV**
4. To hide the details again, click the eye icon again (**隐藏详情** / Hide Details).

---

## Using Your Card Online

Once you have the card details, you can:

- Enter them at any online checkout
- Add the card to **Apple Pay** or **Google Pay**
- Use with any service that accepts Mastercard

---

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Keep your CVV private.** Never share your full card number, expiry date, or CVV with anyone. Stable Mesh support will never ask for these details.
{% endhint %}
