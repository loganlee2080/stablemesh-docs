# Top Up a Card

Move funds from your Stable Mesh crypto wallet onto a specific virtual card so you can start spending.

![Top Up Modal](.gitbook/assets/05_topup_modal_empty.png)

---

## How to Top Up

1. Go to **Cards** and click the card you want to fund.
2. In the detail panel, click the green **Recharge** button.
3. In the modal that appears:
   - **Card** — pre-filled with the selected card name.
   - **Currency** — select `USDT` or `USDC` from the dropdown. Your available wallet balance is shown next to each option.
   - **Amount** — enter the USD amount you want to load onto the card.
4. Click **Recharge** to confirm.

![Top Up Filled](.gitbook/assets/06_topup_modal_filled.png)

---

## What Happens Next

- The amount is **immediately deducted** from your crypto wallet.
- The card's **Available Balance** updates instantly.
- The card is ready to use right away.

---

{% hint style="info" %}
You can only top up using funds currently in your Stable Mesh crypto wallet. If your wallet balance is insufficient, [deposit more crypto](../deposit.md) first.
{% endhint %}
