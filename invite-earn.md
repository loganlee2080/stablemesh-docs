# Invite & Earn

Share Stable Mesh with friends and earn rewards every time someone signs up and starts using the platform through your referral link.

---

## How It Works

1. Click **Invite & Earn** in the top navigation bar.
2. Copy your unique referral link.
3. Share it with friends, colleagues, or your community.
4. When they sign up and use Stable Mesh, **you both earn rewards**.

---

## Tips for Sharing

- Share in crypto communities and Telegram groups
- Include your link in blog posts or social media
- Refer your team if you use Stable Mesh for business expenses

---

Reward terms and rates are shown in the Invite & Earn section of the app. Check there for the latest details on how rewards are calculated and paid out.
