# Dashboard

The **Home** dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of your entire account at a glance.

![Dashboard](.gitbook/assets/01_home_dashboard.png)

---

## Layout

The left sidebar provides navigation across the platform:

| Section | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Home** | Balance overview and recent transactions |
| **Cards** | Manage all your virtual cards |
| **Profile** | Account settings |

---

## Dashboard Sections

### Total Balance

Displays the **combined value** of your crypto wallet holdings and all card balances in USD.

> Total Balance = Crypto Assets + All Card Balances

### Crypto Assets

Shows your on-chain wallet balances for each supported stablecoin:

- **USDT** — Tether USD
- **USDC** — USD Coin

These are the funds available to deposit onto cards.

### USD Cards

A quick summary of all your virtual cards with their current available balance. Click **See All** to go to the full Cards page, or click any card row to open its detail panel.

### Recent Transactions

The latest spending activity across all your cards, showing merchant name, transaction type, and amount.

## Top Actions

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **+ Deposit** | Add crypto to your wallet |
| **Create Card** | Issue a new virtual card |
| **SOL** dropdown | Switch the deposit network |
