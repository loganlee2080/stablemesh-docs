# Getting Started

Welcome to Stable Mesh! Follow these steps to go from sign-up to your first card purchase in minutes.

---

## Step 1 — Sign Up

Visit [app.stablemesh.io](https://app.stablemesh.io) and create your account.

## Step 2 — Deposit Crypto

Before you can use a card, you need funds in your Stable Mesh wallet.

➡️ See [Deposit Crypto](deposit.md) for full instructions.

## Step 3 — Create a Virtual Card

Once your wallet has funds, create your first virtual card.

➡️ See [Create a Card](create-card.md) for full instructions.

## Step 4 — Top Up Your Card

Move funds from your wallet onto the card.

➡️ See [Top Up a Card](cards/top-up.md) for full instructions.

## Step 5 — Start Spending

Use your card number, expiry date, and CVV at any online checkout or add it to Apple Pay / Google Pay.

➡️ See [View Sensitive Info](cards/sensitive-info.md) to reveal your card details.

---

> **Tip:** You can create multiple cards for different purposes — e.g. one for subscriptions, one for travel, one for team expenses.
