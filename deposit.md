# Deposit Crypto

Before you can top up a card, you need funds in your Stable Mesh crypto wallet. Stable Mesh supports USDT and USDC on multiple networks.

---

## How to Deposit

1. Go to the **Home** dashboard.
2. Click the **+ Deposit** button.
3. Select your network from the dropdown (e.g. **SOL** for Solana).
4. Copy your deposit address shown on screen.
5. Send **USDT** or **USDC** from your external wallet to that address.

> ✅ Your balance updates automatically once the transaction is confirmed on-chain. Confirmation time depends on the network.

---

## Supported Networks

| Network | Token |
|---------|-------|
| Solana (SOL) | USDT, USDC |
| Arbitrum (ARB) | USDT, USDC |

> More networks may be available — check the network dropdown in the app for the latest options.

## After Depositing

Once your balance appears under **Crypto Assets** on the dashboard, you're ready to top up a card.
